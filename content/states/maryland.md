# BRIDGE in Maryland

Maryland is one of BRIDGE's founding states. Our weekend programs bring multi-subject academic support and money management education to families across the state — from inner-city Baltimore to suburban Silver Spring.

## Our Impact

Since launching in Maryland, BRIDGE has:

- Served **500+ families** through weekend workshops
- Partnered with **12 community buildings** (schools & churches)
- Trained **45 volunteer educators** across 4 subject areas

## How It Works

Every weekend, BRIDGE volunteers set up in accessible community spaces — schools and churches that families already know and trust. We teach four core subjects:

- **Mathematics** — arithmetic, algebra, and quantitative reasoning
- **Science** — hands-on experiments and critical thinking
- **Literacy** — reading comprehension and writing skills
- **Economics** — budgeting, savings, credit, and long-term planning
